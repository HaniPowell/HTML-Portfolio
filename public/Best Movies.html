The Best Movies According to Hani

Hani's 5-star films.

Wall-E

Two robots falling in love yet it feels more human than 90% of human romances I have seen.

Memories of Murder

A detective movie less about the case than the absolute, irresolvable psychological agony of obsession and failure.

Monster

What at first appears to be a simple story about a parent’s concern for their child’s safety gradually becomes something much richer and poignant. A blistering reminder that we often never fully know what another person is dealing with, especially a child.
